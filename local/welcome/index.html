Welcome to Eynio Connect

Eynio Connect is your first step in turning a home into smart one. Software that allows you to control everything with our apps - Click, Vision or Wall. It will automatically scan your local network looking for supported devices, afterward, you will be able to control them via Eynio Connect or our apps.

Eynio Connect requires a stable computer and a network connection and needs to be ON 24/7, so you can control whatever you want peacefully. And what is the best - it doesn’t require any hardware or wiring.

Whether you have one or one hundred devices to control or locations to monitor, Eynio Connect can allow you full control. To get started click the ‘Set Up Connect’ button.
